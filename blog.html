    What are EAR and APR?

    What are EAR and APR?

    This is equivalent to the rate of interest you'll
    pay if you're overdrawn for a year.
    You'll pay interest on the amount
    you're overdrawn by, and on the interest that
    builds up from being overdrawn. So you'll pay
    less if you regularly pay off your overdraft.
    EAR doesn’t include other fees, like hidden or late fees
    but we don’t charge these anyway.

    APR stands for annual percentage rate.

    It is calculated in the same way as the EAR plus any additional fees (like hidden or late fees) but we don’t charge these anyway so our EAR and APR are the same.
